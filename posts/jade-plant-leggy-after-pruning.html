Pruning and Light Do Different Jobs

This is the thing most people miss, and once you get it, everything clicks:

  • Pruning tells your jade where to grow. When you cut a stem, the plant responds by branching out at that point â€” it creates new growth, new arms, more fullness. Pruning is about shape and structure.
